Meet Stephanie

A quiet authority on the markets that matter in South Florida.

Stephanie Kaufman has earned her place among South Florida's top producers. From Boca Raton to Palm Beach, from oceanfront condominiums to gated golf-and-country-club estates, her clients return — and refer — because she puts the home, the lifestyle, and the long view ahead of the transaction.

Honesty, integrity, and quality. These are the principles that guide her work, and they cannot be compromised. Whether you're a seasoned investor, a snowbird relocating, or buying your first home in Florida — Stephanie meets you where you are and educates you on the market, the community, and the process.
